How it works

This tool uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on flight data to predict arrival delays. The model considers factors like:

  • Seasonality: Time of year affects delay patterns
  • Airport: Different airports have varying delay characteristics
  • Time of day: Rush hours tend to have more delays
  • Distance: Longer flights may have different delay patterns

Predictions are estimates based on historical patterns and may not reflect real-time conditions.
